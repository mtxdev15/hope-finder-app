Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What you carry is sacred. We treat your words, reflections, and prayers with the reverence they deserve.

Who we are

Declare is a product of JC Kingdom Ventures (“Declare,” “we,” “us”), reachable at declareandbelieve.com. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have.

What we collect

  • Account details — your name, email, and country, so you can sign in and we can personalize your experience.
  • What you save — the words, verses, declarations, and prayers you keep in Anchor, plus your chosen translation, text size, and home church.
  • What you enter — the struggles you select or the words you type to receive Scripture. These are used to generate your response.
  • Basic usage — limited, privacy-respecting analytics to keep Declare reliable and improve it.

How we use it

To give you Scripture-rooted words, to save and sync what you keep in Anchor, to remember your preferences, and to keep the service secure. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use the private contents of your reflections to build advertising profiles.

AI-generated content

Your selected struggle or free-text entry is sent securely to our language-model provider to generate your verse, reflection, declarations, and prayer. We minimize what we send and do not attach unnecessary identifying information.

Crisis & safety

Declare never generates content for thoughts of self-harm. If crisis intent is detected, we route you to human help (such as the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline). See Find help now.

Your choices

  • Access, correct, or delete your account from Account.
  • Remove any saved item from Vault at any time.
  • Request a copy or full deletion of your data by emailing us.

Data retention & security

We keep your data only as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service, and we protect it with industry-standard safeguards. No method of transmission is perfectly secure, but we work to protect what you entrust to us.

Children

Declare is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email support@declareandbelieve.com. See also our Terms of Service.
