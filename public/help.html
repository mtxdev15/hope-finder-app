Frequently asked
How does Declare choose a word for me?
When you name what you’re carrying, Declare matches it to Scripture that speaks to that struggle, then offers a reflection, declarations to speak, and a prayer for that moment.
Can I change my Bible translation?
Yes. In The Word, tap the translation selector to switch, or set a default under Account › Appearance & reader. Compare lets you read translations side by side.
How do I save a verse or word?
Tap the bookmark on any word, verse, declaration, or prayer. Everything you save lives in your Vault, ready to return to.
Is Declare free?
Yes. Declare is free for everyone. It’s sustained by the generosity of those who give, so it can stay free for the next person who needs it.
How do I set my home church?
Go to My Church, find a church by name, city, or your location, open it, and tap “Set as My Church.”
