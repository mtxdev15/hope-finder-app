Privacy Policy

Your reflections, prayers, and saved scripture are deeply personal. This explains what we collect, why, and the control you keep over it.

Last updated: May 31, 2026

The short version

Declare & Believe is built to be a private, quiet space for your faith. We collect only what is needed to sign you in and keep your saved content synced. We do not sell your data, and we never share the substance of your journal or prayers.

What we collect

  • Your email address — used only to verify your identity and sign you in.
  • Content you save — verses, declarations, mindsets, prayers, highlights, and journal entries you choose to keep.
  • Basic profile details — your name, country, and a church you optionally save, if you provide them.
  • Light app preferences — theme, reading settings, and translation choices, so the app remembers how you like to read.

How we use it

We use your information to sign you in, sync your saved content across your devices, and remember your preferences. We do not use your journal entries or prayers to train models, advertise to you, or build a profile for marketing.

What we never do

  • We do not sell or rent your personal information.
  • We do not share the content of your journal, prayers, or saved scripture with third parties.
  • We do not show you third-party advertising based on your activity.

Your control

You can edit or delete any saved verse, declaration, mindset, prayer, or journal entry at any time from within the app. You may request that we delete your account and associated data by contacting us at the address on our Contact page.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. No method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure, but we work to safeguard your data and to be honest with you if anything changes.

Questions

If anything here is unclear, please reach out through our Contact page. We would rather answer a question than leave you uncertain.
