About Us

Declare & Believe was built on one conviction: that the words we speak over our lives matter — and that scripture gives us better words to speak.

Why we exist

So many of us carry quiet struggles — shame, fear, comparison, grief — and the voice in our head rarely tells us the truth. We made Declare & Believe to put God’s word back in your mouth: to meet a struggle with scripture, a mindset to overcome it, declarations to speak, and a prayer to pray.

What we believe

  • Death and life are in the power of the tongue. What we declare shapes what we believe.
  • Scripture is for everyone. Reading the Word should be simple, calm, and beautiful — never cluttered.
  • Your inner life is sacred. Your journal and prayers belong to you, and we guard that fiercely.

Who we are

Declare & Believe is created and powered by JC Kingdom Ventures, a small team that builds tools to help people walk closer with God. We are believers building for believers, and we hold this work as ministry first.

Where we’re going

This is just the beginning. We are continually adding translations, deepening the study experience, and listening to the community for what would serve you best. If you have an idea, our door is open on the Contact page.

A word for you

Wherever you are today — weary, hopeful, doubting, or rejoicing — we are glad you’re here. May you declare what is true, and believe it down to your bones.
