For fear & anxiety
Walk through this slowly.
Scripture
“Fear thou not; for I am with thee: be not dismayed; for I am thy God.”
Isaiah 41:10 · KJV
Mindset
Fear says you’re carrying this alone. Scripture answers with a Person, not a pep talk — God himself, near and holding on to you. You don’t have to summon courage; you have to remember who is with you.
So don’t fight the fear with willpower tonight. Turn toward the One who is already here, name what frightens you, and let Him carry what you cannot. His nearness is not a feeling to chase — it is a fact to rest on.
Speak it aloud
I am not alone. God is with me.
I will not be afraid; He holds me.
Prayer
Father, when fear rises, remind me you are here. Steady my heart, and let me rest in your nearness.
Amen.
Be transplanted
Don’t just declare it today
Be rooted in it. Start a 5-day journey and let this truth grow real where fear & anxiety has been — one declaration at a time.
