Version 1.0May 21, 2026
Where it began

The first Declare & Believe — a single, quiet page that meets you with God’s Word the moment you name what you’re carrying. Built on Romans 12:2.

  • Name it, receive the Word
    Pick a struggle or describe it in your own words, and get up to three Bible verses for that exact moment.
  • Explanation, declarations & prayer
    A faith-filled explanation, “I am” declarations to speak aloud, and a personal prayer for what you’re facing.
  • Three translations
    Read it in NKJV, NLT, or NIV — whichever speaks to you best.
  • Copy, share & save
    Take the Word with you, or send it to someone who needs it.
  • Help in crisis
    Immediate access to the 988 Lifeline and Crisis Text Line for anyone in danger — compassion first.
