A calm, premium motion language for Declare & Believe. Reverent, never flashy — Scripture is always the hero; motion only ushers it in. Tuned to ~40% of agency-grade intensity, and it stands fully aside when a reader asks for less.

Easing — the feel of the hand

Nothing moves at a constant speed. Reveals ride an expo-out so they arrive and settle softly; exits accelerate gently away.

Soft — house curve
cubic-bezier(.2,.7,.2,1)
Reveal — expo-out
cubic-bezier(.16,1,.3,1)
Calm — symmetric
cubic-bezier(.4,0,.2,1)
Exit — accelerate
cubic-bezier(.4,0,1,1)

Patterns

Stagger — the cascade

Grouped items reveal one after another, ~90ms apart. This is the heartbeat of the Declare flow: chip → Scripture → reflection → declarations → prayer, each arriving as the last settles. Add data-m-stagger to a parent; children index automatically.

01 Scripture rises first
02 Reflection follows
03 Declarations assemble
04 Prayer closes the movement

The navigation overlay

Not a drawer — a threshold. The background blurs as a full-screen veil fades in and items reveal one by one in large, reverent type. Closing reverses the sequence.

Five movements of the app: Word · Journey · Declare · Vault · You. Declare is the raised gold center — the hero action — with a gently breathing halo. The bar itself rises into place on load.

Accessibility

When a reader asks for less

Under prefers-reduced-motion: reduce, all travel, blur, scale and looping breath are dropped — content arrives with a quick, gentle opacity fade only. Nothing is hidden, nothing moves that could unsettle. Toggle the switch up top to preview it live.
